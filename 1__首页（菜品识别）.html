Avocado salad is a dish. The main ingredients are avocado, apple and toast. The ingredients are corn grain and dried tomato. The production method is to break small pieces of toast, heat them to hard and crisp, cut small pieces of apple, tear small pieces of dried tomato, add various seasonings, split the avocado and fill in the salad.
